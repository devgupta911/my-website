The Universe

Facts About the Universe

Explore the wonders of the cosmos with fascinating facts and celestial objects.

  • Age: The universe is approximately 13.8 billion years old.
  • Composition: Ordinary atoms make up only 4.6%, with the rest being dark matter and dark energy.
  • Size: The observable universe spans about 93 billion light-years in diameter.
  • Origin: The universe began with the Big Bang.
  • Contents: Stars, planets, galaxies, and more.
